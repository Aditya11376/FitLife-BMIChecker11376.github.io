BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator is a simple tool that helps to determine BMI based on weight (kg) and height (cm). BMI is a measure that categorizes a person's body weight relative to their height, helping assess if they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Underweight (less than 18.5) Normal weight (between 18.5 and 24.9) Overweight (between 25 and 29.9) Obese (30 or more)
